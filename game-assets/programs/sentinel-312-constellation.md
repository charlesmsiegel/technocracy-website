## Public face
"312 satellites providing continuous global coverage for communications, observation, and
navigation backup. There is no longer any point on Earth we cannot see." Climate telemetry for
CCRI; navigation backup for 44 governments; "full-spectrum monitoring" for Perimeter Services.
"Anything that moves toward this planet… will be seen, catalogued, and assigned a satisfactory
explanation before it arrives."

## What it actually is
A **planetary Correspondence-and-Dimensional-Science apparatus** disguised as a commercial
smallsat constellation. Its true jobs are Perimeter Services' true jobs: watch every approach
to Earth — orbital, atmospheric, and *dimensional* — for **Threat Null** (see programs/
null-conditioning-screening.md) and Pan-Dimensional Entities, and hand anything anomalous to
"the appropriate directorate." Director Okafor-Cheng's answer about what commissioning observed
("exceeded expectations… being handled by the appropriate directorate") is the horror in plain
sight: the sky is not empty, and now nothing crosses uncounted.

**Mechanics** — treat as networked infrastructure an operative can *task*, not carry:
- **Correspondence 4 (whole-Earth watch):** a tasked Sentinel query treats *any* open-sky
  location on Earth as familiar — effectively no blind spots. A Perimeter analyst rolls
  Enlightenment to resolve a flagged trajectory; the "satisfactory explanation" is often a
  cover-narrative assignment (see procedures/narrative-de-amplification.md).
- **Dimensional Science 2 (Quantum Interference Shielding):** the constellation reinforces the
  Gauntlet along approach corridors, each success adding a point of local Gauntlet — the
  apparatus that keeps "aliens and spirits from touching the material world" at the boundary.
- **Navigation/comms (Data 3):** genuine, and genuinely relied on by 44 governments — which
  makes the whole civilian world dependent on a Void Engineer sensor grid.

## Requisition notes
- **Not requisitionable as a Device.** Tasking priority is HOAR-gated: Perimeter Services and
  Fleet Operations first, CCRI second, "qualified researchers… following standard review"
  (i.e., sanitized products) last. Iteration X's SILENT ANVIL file (ix-05/06) specifically
  *hides* its legacy-carrier contact from the Void Engineers because Sentinel would quarantine
  the channel as Threat Null.
- A cell cannot own Sentinel, but a Void Engineer PC can *task* it — the single most powerful
  find-anyone tool in the campaign, and the fastest way to attract the wrong directorate.

## Story hooks
- Whatever Sentinel-312 saw during commissioning is now a card on the DEEP PERIMETER board. The
  cell can reach it before the directorate closes it.
- The players need to move something across open ground. There is no blind spot — but there is
  a 14-month recalibration window on Copernicus Station (DEEP PERIMETER ve-12) that opens one.
