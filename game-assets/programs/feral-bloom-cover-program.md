## Public face
The **Northern Range Invasive Predator Response Unit**: a legitimate-looking wildlife-management
NGO responding to a new invasive predator. It "now receives genuine federal grant money and
unsolicited job applications, and has been asked to present at a wildlife conference" (pg-10).
"Nothing maintains a cover like an award."

## What it actually is
The containment, recovery, and cover apparatus wrapped around the **THV-9 "Thornveldt"**
uncontained specimen line (see programs/thv-9-thornveldt.md). It is a masterclass in
terranorming a lie into the Consensus by making it *materially real*: the Unit does genuine
field biology, hires genuine biologists (mostly former real biologists whom "this assignment
made the word *were* appropriate," pg-09), and wins genuine grants. Underneath, it runs:

- **Sample recovery** (pg-09): grid sweeps for scat, dens, kill remnants, and cameras — teams
  posing as the cover unit's field biologists.
- **Cover-story management** (pg-03, pg-15): placing "natural hybrid canid" papers and
  de-amplifying inconvenient footage (see procedures/narrative-de-amplification.md).
- **Reclassification pipeline** (pg-12): a standing CLE arrangement routing "unusual predation"
  reports through the Unit before public filing — nine incidents reclassified this quarter as
  "bear, cougar, cougar, bear, bear, dog pack, bear, unknown, and bear," with the "unknown"
  county's coroner **Attuned** (see procedures/adjustment-memory-revision.md).
- **Disclosure control** (pg-14): a former GREEN WILLOW technician who approached a journalist
  about "the 2013 thing" was relocated, given a research post, and given a revised recollection
  of his employment dates.

**Mechanics** — running the Unit as an obstacle:
- It provides Progenitor Damage Control a large **Resources / Influence (Bureaucracy, Media,
  Police)** footprint in the region: any Sleeper channel the players try to use (a coroner, a
  local reporter, a hunting forum, federal grant records) may already be Unit-owned.
- Treat exposure as a race: the Unit's de-amplification and CLE reclassification act *before*
  public filing. Beating it means reaching Sleeper attention faster than the pipeline can
  reclassify.
