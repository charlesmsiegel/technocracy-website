## Public face
To Void Engineer personnel: a mandatory **90-day loyalty and wellness screening**, framed as
routine security hygiene for anyone working the Deep Perimeter. "Verify your screening date
before reading further." Returnees from the Deep Universe pass through quarantine screening as
standard practice.

## What it actually is
The Border Corps' defense against **Null Conditioning** — the co-option effect projected by
**Threat Null** (the adversary that calls itself "the Technocratic Union" and binds victims
*through loyalty itself*, ve-01). Because Threat Null's conditioning works on un-Deprocessed
operatives who trust the Union's forms, the screening exists to catch personnel who have been
turned, and **Deprocessing** exists to inoculate those who must be briefed.

The protocol's grim inversions, straight off the board:
- **Elevated loyalty is a *fail* condition** (ve-09): honest returned crews fail 2–5 instruments
  in week one because the instruments are calibrated to catch fatigue, resentment, and grief —
  all of which honest crews have. A crew that fails *zero* and whose "gratitude toward the Union
  is total" (the DSEV *Cutting Edge* crew, ve-10) is the anomaly. "Do not congratulate them."
- **Conditioning may transmit through recorded media** (ve-06): Cohort C, exposed to 11 seconds
  of filtered adversary audio, is "no longer listed in this file" and has been moved to the
  "Cohort C Continuity Program." The 11-second figure ties to Iteration X's SILENT ANVIL cadence
  study (ix-06) — the Conventions are hiding correlated data from each other.
- **Screening cadence was cut from 180 to 90 days in 2019** on the strength of the "the analogue
  is not absent" hypothesis alone (ve-02), with no positive detection to justify it.

**Mechanics** — running screening as a scene:
- **Detection:** an officer rolls **Mind ●● psychometrics + instrument** vs. the subject's
  ability to feign a *believably imperfect* baseline. A subject who is genuinely fine passes
  with normal human flaws intact; a Null-Conditioned subject is *too* clean, prompt, and uniform
  — success is detecting the *absence* of expected noise. Officer Ashe's method (ve-10):
  introduce deliberate procedural errors and watch whether the crew corrects them.
- **Deprocessing (Mind ●●●):** a counter-conditioning course that strips both Union Social
  Conditioning (see procedures/social-conditioning-refresh.md) *and* adversary Null Conditioning,
  leaving the subject able to be briefed on Threat Null without being converted by the briefing.
  Extended procedure; failure or partial Deprocessing leaves an "attack surface."
- **Consequence:** a false negative seats an adversary asset inside the perimeter. A false
  positive quarantines an innocent returnee for 90–120 days (ve-10, extension pre-authorized).

  personnel cleared for Threat Null disclosure — the Symposium is deliberately not briefed
  (Standing Order 4, ve-01).
- HOAR: Border Corps directorate controls both screening and Deprocessing; results on the
  sensitive cohorts route "to this file only."

## Story hooks
- A Void Engineer PC is overdue for screening. If they have seen something they should not have,
  passing means lying to an instrument built to catch exactly that.
- The *Cutting Edge* crew passed every instrument on day one. If the players can reach them
  before Deprocessing or "Continuity," they are either the campaign's best witnesses to Threat
  Null — or its perfectly camouflaged vanguard.
