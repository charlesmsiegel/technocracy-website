interactions daily. We help our clients know what audiences want before audiences have finished

## What it actually is
The Union's **planetary sentiment-and-Consensus sensor**, built on the Syndicate's data
substrate (Plexic indexing, EDG Virtuous 8 nodes — see wonders/edg-virtuous-8-desire-engine.md)
and read by NWO analysts. It does three Enlightened jobs behind the ad-tech mask:

- **Watch (Correspondence ●●●●):** nine billion interactions/day is whole-population
  Surveillance. On MIRRORGLASS, The Feed provides the "communications overlay" for journalist
  monitoring (nwo-09) and the correlation engine that cut a leak suspect pool from 61 to 9
  (nwo-05).
- **Predict (Entropy ●●●):** "know what audiences want before they finish wanting it" is
  Nonlinear Prediction at scale — the same math the EDG Desire Engine runs per-phone, run per-
  population. It measures **narrative drift**: the leading indicator of a Consensus breach.
- **Detect deviance (Mind ●●):** sentiment reading flags the emotional signatures of an RDE
  spreading — the "emotional urgency" and "explanations that make the world feel larger and
  stranger" that the media-literacy curriculum trains Sleepers to distrust.

The Feed is the **trigger** for the whole suppression stack: it finds the story, then Narrative
De-Amplification (see procedures/narrative-de-amplification.md) kills it.

**Mechanics** — as an apparatus an operative can query:
- **Find-a-person / find-a-narrative:** an NWO analyst rolls Enlightenment (or runs a
  Correspondence ●● Surveillance / Sequencing Procedure) against The Feed to locate a target's
  communications footprint, map contacts, or detect an emerging deviant narrative. Grants a
  large equipment bonus to any Investigation/Intelligence roll about Sleeper information flow.
- **Blind spots:** it sees networked behavior. Targets with genuine tradecraft (Okafor "practices
  better operational security than two of our nine suspects," nwo-02) or who stay off-grid are
  invisible — the campaign's answer to an omniscient surveillance state.
