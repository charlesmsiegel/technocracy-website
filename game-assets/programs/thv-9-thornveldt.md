## Public face
Officially a natural **"northern hybrid canid"** — a coyote-wolf variant — with two placed
papers in cooperative journals and a third being "revised by its author with our editorial
assistance" (pg-03). To Sleeper hunters and ecologists: an unusually clever, unusually tough
new predator spreading through the boreal corridor.

## What it actually is
A gene-edited **chimeric predator chassis** that escaped (or was released) from a Damage
Control-flagged facility:

- **Canid frame** — wolf/coyote base morphology.
- **Cephalopod-derived adaptive dermis** — active camouflage; defeats trail cameras and, per
  MOTHER-4's behavioral file (pg-11), foils capture teams. Loses culture viability within 30
  hours of death ("requires a live host, or at minimum a recently opinionated one," pg-08).
- **Engineered symbiote flora** — provides wound closure and scent masking; the reason the
  animals are hard to track and hard to kill cleanly.
- **Parthenogenic fallback** — a **DORMANT, DO NOT ACTIVATE** design pathway that is *active*
  in the wild population (pg-06). Culling males does not bound the population.
- **Threat-discrimination learning** — a spec "feature": MOTHER-4 distinguishes hunters,
  hikers, and Union recovery teams, and reserves ambush behavior for the recovery teams.

The FERAL BLOOM horror is the founding-date reconciliation: population genetics say **~2013**,
eight years before the official 2021 containment loss, and Damage Control's own procurement
ledger has been quietly buying up evidence since 2014 (pg-05, pg-07). Damage Control has been
buying the escape for a decade.

**Mechanics** (a single THV-9 adult as an antagonist):
- **Physical:** treat as a dire wolf-scale predator — high Strength/Dexterity/Stamina, Brawl
  and Stealth at specialist levels, Perception boosted by symbiote scent-sense.
- **Adaptive dermis:** effectively the **Cloaking**/Chameleon effect while motionless or
  stalking — difficulty to detect it by sight or camera is significantly increased; ambush
  attacks gain surprise unless the target has Life/Prime-assisted senses.
- **Symbiote wound closure:** heals bashing/lethal notably faster than a natural animal; treat
  as regeneration of one health level per turn short of aggravated.
- **Reproduction:** parthenogenic — the population grows even when isolated. A cull is a
  containment problem, not a solution.

## Requisition notes
- **Cannot be requisitioned** — THV-9 is a loose biological, tracked, not issued. Live-capture
  of a breeding designate (MOTHER-4, pg-11) is authorized at AFET 3; on a third failure the
  standing order converts capture to termination.
- Tissue series and specimens are CCC/Damage Control custody only; the 2021 cohort (001–019)
  is incinerated and sealed (pg-13).

## Story hooks
- The genetics say 2013 and the ledger says 2014. If the cell gets the meteorology-group model
  runs (pg-05 hides them there) or the procurement ledger (pg-07), they can prove Damage
  Control concealed an eight-year containment loss from the Symposium.
- MOTHER-4 hunts Union recovery teams specifically. She may be the only "witness" who can lead
  the players back to GREEN WILLOW — if they can follow a predator that hunts the people who
  hunt it.
