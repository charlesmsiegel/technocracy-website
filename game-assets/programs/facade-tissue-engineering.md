## Public face
"Our FACADE tissue-engineering platform has restored full organ function to more than 180,000
patients, with typical regrowth measured in days." Now an insured, outpatient-eligible
procedure through Iridium Medical. Rejection rates "statistically indistinguishable from zero,
as the regrown tissue is, in every measurable sense, the patient's own." "The body already
knows how to build itself. It simply needed clearer instructions."

## What it actually is
A **Life 4** regeneration apparatus productized as a clinic. It genuinely works — the
"clearer instructions" line is an accurate description of a Progenitor Life Effect guiding a
patient's own cells. Zero rejection because it *is* the patient's Pattern. The horror is not in
the medicine but in the **incentive structure and the data**:

- **Iridium's own president says the quiet part** (iridium-regenerative-coverage): "Actuarially,
  a customer with fresh organs is a customer for a very long time." FACADE converts patients
  into long-duration financial assets; "existing members will be contacted proactively… in some
  cases regarding conditions they were not yet aware they had" — i.e., Progenitor diagnostics
  are ahead of the patient's knowledge and are being monetized.
- **Every procedure is archived** (Data 2): 180,000+ patients whose cellular
  baselines, genomes, and outcomes are in Progenitor custody indefinitely (see the Genomic
  Atlas framing on the Progenitors division page — "nothing that lives is ever truly lost to
  us"). A FACADE patient is a permanently sampled, permanently profiled subject.

**Mechanics** — a Progenitor operative with a FACADE field kit:
- **Life 4 regrowth:** heal aggravated damage / rebuild a destroyed organ over an extended
  procedure (canonically days; a skilled operative spending Primal Energy can compress it).
  Coincidental if delivered as "surgery + recovery"; vulgar if witnessed in real time.
- **Diagnostic overreach:** the same platform reads a subject's full biological Pattern —
  useful for identifying Reality Deviants, engineered biology (THV-9 tissue, DermaTrue grafts),
  or a target's hidden conditions.
- **Dependency hook:** a patient "improved" by FACADE can be given a latent condition or a
  telemetry graft (see wonders/dermatrue-synthetic-skin.md) in the same session.

## Requisition notes
- **Not a personal Wonder** in its clinical form; a field kit is HOAR-gated to Progenitor
  medics and clinical amalgams. Patient data feeds the Genomic Atlas and Iridium actuarial
  models.
- Any Device (including an implanted regrown assist) remains Union property post-procedure
  (RSP-3).

## Story hooks
- An ally was "proactively contacted" about a condition they did not know they had. Either the
  Union's diagnostics are that good — or the condition is being installed, not detected.
- A FACADE clinic is the fastest way to heal a mortally wounded PC ally, and the fastest way to
  put them permanently on file. The players have to decide.
