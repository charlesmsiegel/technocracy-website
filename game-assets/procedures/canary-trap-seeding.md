## Public face
None — this is an internal counterintelligence method, never spoken of outside the Watchers.

## What it actually is
The classic **canary trap**, run at Union scale: seed each suspect with a uniquely watermarked
variant of a sensitive document — identical except for the ordering of two clauses and the
value of one invented statistic — then watch which variant surfaces in the wild. When it does,
its recipient list is one name long. On MIRRORGLASS, "wave 3" seeded **22 variants** across a
residual suspect pool of nine; variant 17 surfaced in journalist Okafor's draft folder within
nine days.

**Mechanics.**
- **Construction (mundane):** an extended **Intelligence + Bureaucracy** (or Subterfuge) roll
  to build N indistinguishable variants; successes govern how subtle and how numerous the
  discriminators are. One variant per suspect.
- **Detection:** monitor outlets, drafts, and refuse (see procedures/narrative-de-amplification
  .md for The Feed monitoring); when a variant appears, attribution is automatic.
- **Enlightened assist (optional Adjustment):**
  - **Correspondence ●● (Tracking Device):** treat each seeded document as a planted focus —
    three successes let a Watcher passively track the physical document as a "familiar
    possession," tightening chain-of-custody reconstruction (nwo-12).
  - **Entropy ● (Nonlinear Prediction):** pre-rank suspects by who is *most likely* to leak,
    shrinking the pool before a single variant moves.
- **The failure mode is the plot:** MIRRORGLASS variant 17 surfaced carrying variant 17's
  statistic but variant 16's clause ordering — meaning the leak passes through two hands, or
  someone is editing for style. A canary trap catches *a* leaker; it does not catch a *chain*.

## Requisition notes
- **Not gear.** A standing Watcher method. Constructing a wave requires Ivory Tower
  authorization and access to a genuinely sensitive document to mutilate.
- The Enlightened tracking assist is HOAR-gated only in that it consumes a Correspondence-
  capable operative's time; the paper trick itself is available to any competent analyst.

## Story hooks
- The players are handed a "leaked" Union document. It is a seeded variant, and reading it
  paints a target on whoever they got it from.
- MIRRORGLASS's chain has two hands, and the second one is a Watcher editing for style. If the
  cell can reach that person before the investigation does, they have a source inside the NWO.
