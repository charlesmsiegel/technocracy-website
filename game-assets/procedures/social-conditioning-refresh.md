## Public face
Internally styled as "wellness screening," "verification interviews," and "Conditioning
refresh." Staff "return calm and content"; "improved sleep." A benefits perk. The word
*refresh* does enormous work.

## What it actually is
The maintenance dose of canonical **Social Conditioning** — the permanent NWO indoctrination
that makes agents obey Control without question — reapplied to personnel whose loyalty has
drifted or who have simply *seen too much*. On MIRRORGLASS it is used offensively: the whole
Records Annex is scheduled for refresh so the leak investigation's reveal "cannot be turned
back on us," and so a colleague under suspicion watches everyone else come back placid before
their own turn.

**Mechanics** (canonical):
1. **Break temporary Willpower:** roll **Enlightenment vs. Willpower + 3**. Success destroys
   temporary Willpower.
2. **Accumulate Conditioning:** once temporary Willpower is gone, bank **one resisted success
   per day** up to the desired Conditioning level (each level = one more layer of
   unquestioning obedience / one more erased objection).
- **Refresh vs. fresh install:** a refresh targets an already-conditioned subject; it re-seats
  slipped Conditioning and can be paired with **Mind ●●● memory edit** (see procedures/
  adjustment-memory-revision.md) so the subject "no longer remembers why they needed it."
- **Delivery/blatancy:** framed as therapy or a routine interview, it is a coincidental
  **Adjustment**. If the subject knows what is happening and resists openly, it becomes a
  vulgar **Procedure** and can draw Paradox.
- **Deprocessing** is the counter — the Void Engineers run it (see programs/
  null-conditioning-screening.md); it is why an un-Deprocessed briefed operative is "an attack
  surface."

## Requisition notes
- Not gear; a **standing NWO capability**. Scheduling authority sits with Ivory Tower
  Supervisors (nwo-08 assignee: "Supervisor 9 (Ivory Tower)"). HOAR-gated: refreshing a
  *fellow operative* above your SDS grade requires directorate sign-off.
- Applied to Sleepers, the mission-length version is **Programming** (Mind ●●●) — five-minute
  brainwash, one simple-sentence command per success over Willpower.

- Someone survived a refresh with their memories intact and is faking contentment. They are
  the best asset the players will ever have, and the shortest-lived.
