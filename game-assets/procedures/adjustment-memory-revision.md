## Public face
In the Lexicon, an **Adjustment** is simply "a subtle or coincidental act of Enlightened
Science." In HR-speak it is a "voluntary" resolution, a "revised recollection," a colleague
who is "doing very well." No one is ever told they were edited; that is what makes it an
Adjustment rather than a Procedure.

## What it actually is
The Union's everyday tool for making an inconvenient memory, belief, or witness go quietly
away — the low-blatancy cousin of Processing (procedures/processing.md), applied to a single
person without a facility. Two field flavors appear on the boards:

- **NWO memory revision ("Adjustment"):** **Mind 3** erases recent memory (canonically a
  Mind 3 Psychiatric Compounds effect) and rewrites a plausible substitute. Suspect 44 (nwo-13)
  had independently deduced the canary-trap investigation; her *analysis* was retained in the
  file, her *recollection of writing it* was not. A promotion and relocation launder the gap.
- **Progenitor "Attunement":** the biochemical route — **Mind 2 + Life 2** (Rats' Rage /
  Lost Cousin), "scenting" a subject with pheromones or nanotech to reshape their disposition,
  or Psychiatric Compounds to dull skepticism. The "unknown"-county coroner (pg-12) was Attuned
  into signing off on a THV-9 kill as an ordinary animal attack.

**Mechanics.**
- Roll **Enlightenment vs. Willpower** (add Manipulation dice for a convincing social frame).
  Successes set how much can be edited: **1 success** buries a detail (a phone number, a face);
  **3+** rewrites an episode; **10** approaches full amnesia (per Purge Thought Crime scaling).
- **Prime 2** makes it stick; without it, buried memory can resurface under stress or trigger.
- **Blatancy:** coincidental so long as the subject accepts the new story. A subject who
  *knows* they are being edited and resists forces a vulgar contest and risks Paradox.
- **Counter:** strong Willpower, an external record the edit missed, or a Mind-shielded mind.
  Recalling purged information floods the subject with "psychic static" and a headache.

## Requisition notes
- **Not gear.** A standing capability of any Mind 3-capable NWO operative or Progenitor
  Pharmacopoeist. "Voluntary Adjustment" language in a case file means the subject was not, in
  any meaningful sense, asked.
- HOAR note: Adjusting a fellow operative or a public official (a coroner, a journalist)
  crosses into CCC territory and is logged — see the reassignment cards on every board.

## Story hooks
- An ally has a hole in their memory where the cell used to be. External evidence — a photo, a
  note in their own hand (nwo-13's retained notes) — could break the edit open.
- The Attuned coroner is starting to have the headaches. He signed something he cannot
  remember, and he wants to know what.
