## Public face
None external. Internally, "Processing" is a bureaucratic euphemism of the blandest possible
kind — a captured hostile is "submitted for Processing," and emerges as compliant Union
personnel or a wiped, released Sleeper. On sy-11 two captured **Project Invictus** operatives
(a private security force that raided an SISD site believing it was SPD) are Processed as a
matter of routine; "their initiative has been noted favorably in files they will never see."

## What it actually is
The Union's standard end-state for a captured, un-Enlightened or low-value hostile who is not
worth killing but cannot be released as-is. It is **Social Conditioning + memory revision**
run in a controlled facility rather than the field:

- **Strip resistance:** **Mind ●●●** (with the canonical Psychiatric Compounds, **Life ●●● +
  Mind ●●**, to chemically dull "resistance to suggestion") breaks the subject's Willpower.
- **Overwrite:** **Mind ●●●** erases the operation, the raid, and often the subject's prior
  loyalties; **Prime ●●** sets the result.
- **Re-task or release:** the subject is folded into the Union as an obedient asset, or
  released as a Sleeper with a plausible false history (a "laboratory accident," a new job, a
  revised recollection of employment dates — cf. the GREEN WILLOW technician, FERAL BLOOM
  pg-14, and the Adjusted archivist, MIRRORGLASS nwo-13).

**Mechanics.** Treat as an **extended** procedure over days in a facility:
- Roll **Enlightenment vs. Willpower + 3** to break temporary Willpower (Psychiatric Compounds
  and restraint let the Storyteller drop the +3 or grant automatic successes).
- Then bank one resisted success/day to install the desired obedience/false-memory state.
- **Blatancy:** entirely coincidental *from the subject's future perspective* — they will
  remember therapy, a job offer, an accident. Vulgar only if witnessed mid-process by an
  Enlightened outsider.

## Requisition notes
- **Not requisitionable gear.** It is a **standing procedure** triggered by intake (sy-11:
  "per standing procedure"). Requires a secure S&C facility and NWO/Progenitor clinical support.
- Reserved for captured hostiles and personnel liabilities. Applying it to a fellow operative
  above grade is a directorate-level decision.

## Story hooks
- The Invictus operatives had families and employers who think they simply vanished. One
  family hired someone to look — that someone finds the cell.
- A Processed hostile the players once fought is now a smiling Union clerk who does not know
  them. Something in the overwrite is starting to fail.
