## Public face
"Our lab-grown dermal substrate is now the clinical standard for burn reconstruction on four
continents. Indistinguishable from natural tissue in 99.97% of blinded evaluations." A
triumph of regenerative medicine, donated at scale, wrapped around every BioMechanics limb.

## What it actually is
A cultured living-tissue sheet grown from a Progenitor **Life 2** template — genuinely
indistinguishable from natural skin because, at the Pattern level, it *is* skin (rejection
rates statistically indistinguishable from zero for the same reason FACADE tissue is: it is
matched to the recipient). Two things the copy omits:

- **The remaining 0.03%.** The blinded evaluators who flagged a graft were not wrong. The
  standard sheet is inert; a **Life 3** "clinical telemetry" variant carries dormant
  sensor flora that reports galvanic response, temperature, and gross motion back through
  the recipient's registered limb or implant. This is the layer that makes a burn victim a
  permanently instrumented data source.
- **Graft as focus.** A Progenitor operative wearing DermaTrue can justify Life Effects
  (rapid healing, disguise via **Life 3** minor shapechange, toxin resistance) as
  "the material doing its job." Coincidental while it heals; vulgar if the skin visibly
  flows.

**Mechanics.** A standard graft is cosmetic and grants no dice. The telemetry variant lets a
monitoring Progenitor roll **Perception + Medicine** (or a Sequencing-style Data
Procedure) to read the wearer's vitals and coarse location at any range the site's networks
reach. An operative-grade implant layer grants +1 soak against bashing/fire and lets the
wearer spend a Willpower to attempt a coincidental **Life 2** self-heal once per scene.

## Requisition notes
- **RSP catalog code:** PG-DERM-2 (cosmetic) / PG-DERM-3T (telemetry). Cosmetic grafts are
  open-issue to any medical amalgam. The telemetry variant is HOAR-restricted: issuing it to
  a *recipient* is routine; issuing it to a *field operative* requires Progenitor sign-off
  because the wearer is then also a leak.
- Civilian donation stock is unlimited by design — every donated graft is a registered node.

## Story hooks
- A player-side contact received a "charity" graft years ago. When the cell trips a Sentinel
  or Feed flag, Progenitors already have their pulse and position.
- The 0.03% who "failed" blind evaluation are being quietly re-contacted for "follow-up." One
  of them figured out why and is trying to cut the skin off.
