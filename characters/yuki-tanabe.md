## Merits & Flaws
- **Merit: Enhanced Recovery** — Field injuries that would sideline a normal medic put Yuki back on the line in days.
- **Flaw: Adjusted Fear Response** — The chemistry that lets Yuki work three feet from an apex chimeric predator also means Yuki's instincts no longer fire when they should. The teams that failed capture attempt two lost their nerve. Yuki lost the ability to.

## Description & Demeanor
Bright, personable, tirelessly capable — the medic everyone on the team trusts and the one who keeps morale alive on month-long sweeps. Yuki genuinely loves the fieldwork and only occasionally notices that the "invasive predator response" they signed up for keeps producing specimens that recognize the difference between hunters and hikers.

## What They Want
To keep every member of the team breathing through capture attempt three, and to publish, someday, the extraordinary animal they are absolutely not allowed to describe.

## Secret
Yuki has patched up a THV-9 juvenile once, on reflex, in the field — sealed its wound with the same kit used on teammates — and hid the incident from the necropsy ledger. Yuki cannot decide whether that was a betrayal of the mission or the only honest thing they've done since taking it.
