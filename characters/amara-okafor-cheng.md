## Merits & Flaws
- **Merit: Acute Vision / Perfect Peripheral Awareness** — She notices the object that is not supposed to be there before the algorithm flags it.
- **Flaw: Compulsion (Accounting)** — She physically cannot leave a trajectory unresolved; an unexplained object costs her sleep until it is catalogued, whatever the catalogue has to say.

## Description & Demeanor
Calm, curious, disciplined — she embodies the division's hiring line ("the curious, the courageous, and the calm"). She smiles when asked what the constellation has seen and says the data is "being handled by the appropriate directorate," and she means it as reassurance, mostly to herself. She bears the weight of a full sky lightly in public and not at all in private.

## Description continued
She commands from telemetry, not a bridge, and treats her Perimeter Services crews as family she is trying very hard to bring home.

## What They Want
A fully accounted-for sky — every object seen, catalogued, and assigned a satisfactory explanation before it arrives, so that the frontier only ever surprises humanity "in the fun ways."

## Secret
The DEEP PERIMETER file (ve-02) records that there is no Void Engineer mirror-faction in THREAT NULL's order of battle — and Hypothesis 3 says the reason may be that such a mirror would be "indistinguishable from Void Engineer personnel operating at extended range." Her own screening date is thirty-one days past due, and she has been quietly resequencing the fleet's screening schedule so that hers keeps landing last.
