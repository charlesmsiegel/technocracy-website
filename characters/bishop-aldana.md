# Bishop Aldana

**Nature:** Guardian | **Demeanor:** Caregiver

## Attributes
- **Physical** — Strength 5*, Dexterity 4*, Stamina 5* (*augmented; see Enhancement)
- **Social** — Charisma 2, Manipulation 2, Appearance 2
- **Mental** — Perception 4, Intelligence 3, Wits 4

## Abilities
- **Talents** — Alertness 4, Athletics 3, Brawl 4, Intimidation 3, Awareness 2
- **Skills** — Firearms 4, Melee 3, Drive 3, Larceny 4 (Security Systems), Survival 2, Technology 2
- **Knowledges** — Investigation 3, Science 1, Esoterica (Enlightened Science) 0 — uses hypertech, does not understand it

## Enhancement & Gear (in place of Spheres)
- **Enhancement 4** — Myoelectric arm and legs with full haptic feedback and DermaTrue™ synthetic skin (BioMechanics Access Program hardware), subdermal impact plating, an Implant Radio, and telemetered lifetime calibration. Bishop's limbs are "registered, telemetered, and continuously improved through over-the-air refinement" — which is to say the Convention can see, and adjust, exactly where Bishop is at all times.
- **Signature gear** — X-5 Protector sidearm, Alanson light hardsuit (issued for high-alert windows), sensor contacts.
- **No Genius, no Spheres.** Bishop wields Iteration X hypertech as a trained user, not a maker; the devices do the impossible parts.

## Willpower
7

## Backgrounds
- Enhancement 4, Requisitions 2, Backup 2, Resources 2

## Merits & Flaws
- **Merit: Loyalty (earned)** — The Program gave Bishop back the ability to walk and hold a child; the gratitude is real, not conditioned.
- **Flaw: Telemetered** — Bishop's own body reports to the Convention. Bishop knows this and has made a kind of peace with it. The peace is load-bearing.

## Description & Demeanor
Broad, quiet, unfailingly gentle for someone who can put a fist through a bulkhead — the calm of a person who nearly lost everything and got it back on someone else's terms. Bishop treats the graveyard-shift cipher (Iterator 7731-Beta) with protective courtesy and brings her coffee she never asked for. Off shift, Bishop coaches a kids' wheelchair basketball league and does not connect the two facts.

## What They Want
To keep the people inside the wire safe, to keep the calibration warranty current, and never to have to ask what the ground station is actually listening for.

## Secret
The "continuous over-the-air refinement" of Bishop's limbs occasionally overrides them for a fraction of a second — a twitch, a grip that tightens on its own. Bishop has felt it, filed no report, and privately schedules the yearly recalibration for the week after visiting family, so that if the Convention is steering, at least it isn't steering then.
