# Consensus Holdings — NPC Roster (Storyteller Material)

> **This folder is Storyteller reference for a *Mage: the Ascension 20th Anniversary* (M20)
> Technocracy campaign. It is deliberately kept out of `src/` and `public/` and is NOT served by
> the site build.** The public website is the players' in-fiction artifact — the corporate face of
> Consensus Holdings. These sheets are the machine behind that face: the people who wrote the press
> releases, staffed the kanban boards, and posted the careers listings the players can actually
> read on the site. Every NPC here is cross-referenced to where they appear in the site data, so a
> player who digs into the fiction is always digging toward a real person.

The tone target throughout: **competent professionals inside a quietly sinister machine —
sympathetic, not cartoonish.** Even the receptionist has a secret. The janitor's is the point.

## The three tiers
- **Enlightened Technocrats** — the named executives and board assignees, statted with full M20
  blocks: Nature/Demeanor, Attributes, Abilities, Enlightenment (the Technocratic Arete), Spheres
  (Technocratic names — **Data** for Correspondence, **Dimensional Science** for Spirit; Prime and
  the rest keep their names, and Quintessence is styled **Primal Energy**), Focus (Paradigm /
  Practice / Tenets / Instruments), Genius/Paradox notes, Resonance, Willpower, Primal Energy, and
  Backgrounds. All five Conventions are covered. Stat blocks follow the Faces-of-Magick NPC
  convention: treat every number as *suggested* and scale to the chronicle — but Sphere ratings
  never exceed Enlightenment.
- **Extraordinary Citizens** — unEnlightened but exceptional personnel (T1): cyborg security,
  Progenitor-adjusted field medic, hypertrained analyst, sensor-prodigy operator. Attributes and
  Abilities plus **Enhancement** dots and special gear — no Spheres.
- **Sleeper employees** — genuinely mundane staff (T0) who keep the company running and suspect
  little or nothing. Simple blocks, one quirk each, and a secret.

## Naming & consistency notes for the ST
- Titles and roles match the site exactly. If a press release says *"Executive Comptroller of
  Iteration X ... Cornelius Vasquez,"* the sheet is Vasquez with that title.
- **Two Vosses, no relation.** Adrian Voss (Syndicate VPO, press release) and the LEDGER assignee
  *Comptroller Voss, L.* (board `syndicate` card `sy-09`) are different people; the coincidence
  annoys both and is a usable red herring. Only Adrian Voss is statted here.
- Named execs referenced in the fiction but not yet statted (fair game to build next): Margaret
  Hale (Chief Continuity Officer), Dr. Priya Ananda-Okonkwo (DOAA Research Director), Dr. Henrik
  Osei (Progenitor CCO), Dr. Elias Wren (BioMechanics Program Comptroller), Sable Laurent (EDG
  Creative Director), Dominic Ferro (Mercury CEO), Celeste Marchand (Iridium President), and the
  freelance journalist L. Okafor (an adversary NPC, not an employee).
- Rank uses the Six-Tier Pyramid (T0–T5); loyalty uses the Six Degrees of Separation (SDS 1–7,
  where lower is more loyal); discretion uses the Damian Scale (1–10, keep it low). Enlightened
  Effects are framed as **Adjustments** (subtle/coincidental) and **Procedures** (the big, vulgar
  guns), per M20's Enlightened Science framing.
