## Description & Demeanor
Tired-eyed, dry-humored, meticulous; she has interviewed enough honest grief to recognize its absence instantly. She treats the Cutting Edge crew with wary courtesy and has been granted authority to seed deliberate errors into their schedule "and observe whether the crew corrects them." She does not congratulate anyone for elevated loyalty. That is a flag condition.

## What They Want
To be *certain* — to find the single instrument the perfect crew will fail, and to send home only the people who are actually the people who left.

## Secret
On day one she introduced a procedural error the Cutting Edge crew corrected before she had finished writing it down — corrected it in her own handwriting, in a form she had not yet issued. She logged the correction and has told no one, because the only honest conclusion is that something on this station already knows how she works.
