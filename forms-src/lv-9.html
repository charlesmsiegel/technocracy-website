NOTICES.
  1. All personnel on leave remain subject to recall to duty at any moment, including moments you have already experienced. Personnel recalled retroactively will be compensated retroactively.
  2. Leave under item 7d accrues at the rate experienced. Personnel returning before they departed should report to Payroll before doing either.
  3. Unused annual leave does not carry over. It reverts to the Union, which will experience it on your behalf.
  4. Personnel selecting item 7g are reminded that reflection is most productive in a structured setting. Structured settings are available. Attendance may be arranged without further paperwork.
  5. Failure to return from leave on the date at item 9 constitutes Dereliction of Duty (Orange Handbook §12.6). The Union has never lost an operative to leave. Operatives on leave are not lost. Their locations are known.
  6. Enjoy your leave! Rest is a Union asset entrusted to you for maintenance.
