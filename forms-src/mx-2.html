NOTICES.
  1. Self-reporting within 72 hours of exposure is treated as Neglect at worst (Orange Handbook §12.2). Reporting after 72 hours is still encouraged, and still counted.
  2. A negative screening result clears the operative; it does not clear the meme. Cleared personnel may notice reduced recollection of Part II. This is the treatment working.
  3. Reporting a colleague's symptoms requires Form MX-3 (Memetic Exposure Report, Third Party), available at all Cognitive Hygiene stations, break rooms, and exits.
  4. Do not discuss this filing with amalgam-mates, including to reassure them. Reassurance is a vector.
  5. Personnel who have filed three or more MX-2 reports in a calendar year qualify for the Sentinel Award (lapel pin; monitored).
